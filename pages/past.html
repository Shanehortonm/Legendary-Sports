The Hall of Fame

Deep within the heart of Canton, OH lives the NFL's Pro Football Hall of Fame, a constant reminder that through blood, sweat and tears anyone can become a legend.

As of 2022 The Dallas Cowboys have produced 29 Hall of Famers in total. It is a important to note that 10 of these legends had only spent the last few seasons of their career as a Cowboy before retiring and later being inducted into the Hall of Fame.

  • Tony Dorsett | RB | 1977 - 1987

  • Harold Carmichael | WR | 1984
  • Mike Ditka | TE | 1969 - 1972
